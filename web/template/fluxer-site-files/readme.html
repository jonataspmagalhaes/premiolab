Fluxer AI Website Builder Template

Build, launch, and scale your creative platform with a modern, high-performance SaaS template designed for AI-first products.

Fluxer is a sleek, conversion-driven website template crafted for platforms focused on AI tools, digital marketplaces, and creative communities. With a bold visual style, smooth interactions, and structured content flow, it helps you present your product with clarity and impact, from first impression to sign-up.

Perfect for startups, creators, and founders building in AI, design tools, or digital ecosystems.

⚠️ Note

This template is designed for demonstration purposes. All content, names, and media are placeholders and can be fully customized.

What’s Included

A complete multi-page experience designed to guide users from discovery to conversion:

  • Home — High-impact hero, product highlights, and social proof
  • Fluxer AI — Showcase your AI features, workflows, and capabilities
  • Marketplace — Display digital assets, templates, or tools in a clean grid system
  • Tutorials — Educational content layout to onboard and engage users
  • Events — Promote workshops, launches, or community sessions
  • Pricing — Flexible pricing plans with comparison and clear CTAs
  • Log in — Clean authentication entry for users
