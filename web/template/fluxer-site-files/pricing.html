Pricing FAQs

A seat represents a single user license within your team. Different seat types (Collab, Dev, Full) grant different levels of access to Fluxer's tools. You can mix and match seat types within your team.

You can view your current seat usage and estimated billing in the Admin Dashboard under the "Billing" tab. We provide a detailed breakdown of each user's seat type.

You create a team workspace and add members. You are billed for the total number of paid seats in that workspace at the end of your billing cycle.
